You will need to deploy following files to your smx4/deploy folder.
They are needed for spring-dm osgi compatibility and bean injection.

You can get these files from :
http://www.springsource.org/download


--- Spring Dynamic Modules 1.2.1 ---
spring-osgi-annotation-1.2.1.jar
spring-osgi-core-1.2.1.jar
spring-osgi-extender.1.2.1.jar
spring-osgi-io.1.2.1.jar
spring-osgi-web-1.2.1.jar
spring-osgi-web-extender.1.2.1.jar

--- Spring Framework 2.5.6.SEC01 ---
spring-web.jar
spring-webmvc.jar
